About Red Cross Club

The Red Cross Club is a service-based organization that brings students together to support our school and community through meaningful volunteer opportunities. Members participate in blood drives, disaster preparedness education, fundraising, and other humanitarian projects inspired by the mission of the American Red Cross. Our goal is to develop leadership, promote compassion, and make a positive difference in the lives of others.
